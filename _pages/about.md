I am Yifan Cai, a fifth-year Ph.D. student advised by Prof. [Linh Thi Xuan Phan](https://www.cis.upenn.edu/~linhphan/) at the University of Pennsylvania. I got my bachelor's degree from the School of Software at Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2020. My projects mainly focus on achieving dependability in resource-constrained systems, including traditional distributed systems and real-time cyber-physical systems (CPS).
